## High-Risk Gates

| Gate | Status | Why It Matters | Immediate Action |
|---|---|---|---|
| Administrative compliance pack complete | not started | Missing items may eliminate the bid | Build and review compliance pack first |
| Pricing compliant with no-license-cost rule | not started | Non-compliant pricing may invalidate response | Validate pricing structure before commercial review |
| DHA connectivity path confirmed for prototype | blocked | Prototype scoring depends on working access path | Escalate dependency with customer-facing team |
| Technical evidence pack complete | not started | Scored evaluation expects supporting artifacts | Produce diagrams, risk assessment, and security plan |
| Named team and reference evidence complete | not started | Stage 1B scoring depends on proof, not narrative | Collect CVs, qualifications, reference letters, and award letters |

| Review Point | Recommended Timing | Outcome |
|---|---|---|
| Compliance readiness review | First | Confirm mandatory forms and evidence path |
| Technical evidence review | Second | Confirm architecture, security, and prototype readiness |
| Commercial readiness review | Third | Confirm pricing and preference-point compliance |
| Final packaging review | Last | Confirm full submission set is complete and consistent |
