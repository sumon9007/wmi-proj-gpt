# Key Requirements

## Functional Requirements

- provide a web-based biometric authentication platform
- support desktop and mobile-capable access
- support biometric and identity-number-based verification workflows
- integrate with DHA for identity verification
- integrate with credit bureau services for demographic, address, employment, and business-interest checks
- support banking validation and payroll-related verification use cases
- provide reporting, dashboards, exception handling, and audit trails

## Technical Requirements

- align to a SITA-managed Windows-based environment
- use a controlled integration layer or ESB model
- support secure storage, logging, backup, and DR
- support compatible biometric capture devices and approved equivalents
- enable real-time and bulk verification patterns

## Security And Compliance Requirements

- align to POPIA and GPT governance expectations
- enforce RBAC and MFA
- encrypt data at rest and in transit
- provide audit logging and monitoring
- support DR targets of RTO 4 hours and RPO 15 minutes
- support service availability expectations of 99.5 percent uptime excluding scheduled maintenance

## Commercial And Governance Requirements

- no third-party license cost for use of the delivered system
- GPT owns data, source code, configuration, and project-generated IP
- the solution must support government control and broader use across government structures without royalty-style restrictions

## Delivery Requirements

- complete implementation, deployment, and training within 12 months
- provide support and maintenance through month 36
- supply documentation, training, and handover material
- demonstrate capability through a prototype or demo stage
