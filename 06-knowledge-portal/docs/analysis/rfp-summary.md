# RFP Summary

## RFP Details

- RFP / Tender Name: Development of Biometric Authentication System Including Support and Maintenance for Period of 3 Years
- Tender Number: GT/GPT/009/2026
- Customer: Gauteng Provincial Treasury, on behalf of Gauteng Provincial Government
- Opportunity Type: Public-sector implementation and support tender
- Contract Duration: 36 months

## Executive Summary

GPT is seeking a bidder to design, build, implement, and support a web-based biometric authentication platform for use across Gauteng government departments and municipalities. The system is intended to improve recruitment screening, payroll cleansing, supplier due diligence, beneficiary verification, and fraud prevention through real-time verification against the Department of Home Affairs, credit bureau services, banking-related verification processes, and internal government systems.

The opportunity is not just an application build. It is a controlled government platform engagement with strict hosting, security, data ownership, auditability, compliance, resilience, and documentation obligations. The winning bidder must also demonstrate delivery credibility through a prototype, qualifications, references, and strong administrative compliance.

## Customer Goals

- improve the accuracy and integrity of employee, supplier, and beneficiary verification
- reduce fraud, adverse audit findings, and manual processing
- enable real-time decision support using biometric and demographic verification data
- centralize control of system access, data, and operating governance under GPT
- modernize service delivery across multiple departments while preserving public-sector compliance

## In-Scope Capabilities

- web-based biometric authentication system
- desktop and mobile capability
- fingerprint and facial recognition support
- DHA integration for identity verification
- credit bureau integration for demographic, employment, address, and business-interest verification
- banking details validation capability
- Persal-related payroll integration
- real-time and bulk verification
- reporting, dashboards, audit trails, and exception reports
- ESB or integration layer for external and internal system connectivity
- training, documentation, support, and maintenance

## Evaluation Overview

- Stage 1A: administrative compliance
- Stage 1B: technical evaluation with a minimum threshold
- Stage 1C: demonstration or prototype presentation with a minimum threshold
- Stage 2: price and preference evaluation

## Proposal Implications

- the response must be administratively compliant, not just technically strong
- the architecture must make hosting, control, security, resilience, and integration constraints explicit
- the implementation plan must show a credible 12-month delivery path and 24-month support model
- a prototype is part of competitiveness, not optional polish
