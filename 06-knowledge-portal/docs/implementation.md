# Implementation

## Delivery Phases

The delivery plan is structured into two phases.

### Phase 1: Implementation

Months 1-12 cover:

- mobilization and governance setup
- discovery and requirements validation
- solution and security design
- build and configuration
- DHA, credit bureau, banking, and payroll-related integration work
- testing and prototype readiness
- training, handover, and go-live stabilization

### Phase 2: Support And Maintenance

Months 13-36 cover:

- support and incident handling
- controlled updates and fixes
- operational monitoring and reporting
- maintenance of integrations and environment alignment
- service review and continual improvement

## Major Milestones

- project mobilization completed
- requirements and architecture baseline approved
- environment readiness confirmed
- DHA and priority integrations validated
- build and core workflows completed
- testing and training completed
- go-live completed within 12 months
- transition into support phase completed

## Core Workstreams

- project management and governance
- business analysis and process validation
- architecture and security design
- application build and configuration
- integration delivery
- testing and quality assurance
- training and operational handover
- support transition

## First 90 Days

The first 90 days should focus on de-risking dependencies rather than rushing build output.

Priority actions:

- confirm governance model, stakeholders, and decision cadence
- validate environment and SITA readiness assumptions
- confirm DHA access path and approved IP arrangements
- baseline security, logging, backup, and DR design expectations
- clarify credit bureau, banking, and payroll-related integration details
- lock down prototype scope and demonstration path

## Dependencies And Assumptions

The implementation plan depends on several customer-controlled inputs:

- DHA connectivity through GPT-approved network arrangements
- SITA environment readiness
- customer stakeholder availability for reviews and sign-off
- technical clarification for banking and payroll-related interfaces
- GPT IT participation in training and handover

These are not minor assumptions. They sit on the implementation critical path and need active tracking from mobilization onward.
