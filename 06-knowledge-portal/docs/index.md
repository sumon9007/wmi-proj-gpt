# Gauteng Treasury Knowledge Portal

This portal summarizes the Gauteng Provincial Treasury biometric authentication opportunity and the current solution and delivery position prepared by Waymark Infotech.

It is intended for internal reuse, onboarding, and project continuity. It focuses on business context, architecture, implementation, operations, key decisions, and practical team questions.

## Project Snapshot

- Customer: Gauteng Provincial Treasury, on behalf of Gauteng Provincial Government
- Bidder: Waymark Infotech
- Tender: GT/GPT/009/2026
- Opportunity: Development of Biometric Authentication System Including Support and Maintenance for a Period of 3 Years
- Status: Pre-award / proposal stage

## What Matters Most

- The solution must operate in a SITA-managed Windows environment.
- GPT retains control of data, access, governance, and project-generated IP.
- Delivery is split into 12 months of implementation and 24 months of support.
- DHA, credit bureau, banking, and payroll-related integrations are central to success.
- Security, POPIA alignment, resilience, and auditability are core design requirements, not side topics.
