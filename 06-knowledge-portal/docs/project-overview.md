# Project Overview

## Business Context

Gauteng Provincial Treasury is procuring a biometric authentication platform to strengthen recruitment screening, payroll cleansing, supplier due diligence, beneficiary verification, and fraud prevention across Gauteng Provincial Government. This is a public-sector control and modernization initiative rather than a standalone application build.

The solution must support real-time verification against the Department of Home Affairs, credit bureau services, banking validation processes, and payroll-related workflows, while preserving central government control over data, access, and operations.

## Customer Problem Summary

The customer is trying to address a mix of operational and governance pain points:

- fragmented and manual verification processes
- weak control over employee, supplier, and beneficiary validation
- fraud and adverse-audit risk
- poor data quality across payroll and verification-sensitive processes
- limited cross-department consistency and traceability

## Scope At A Glance

- web-based biometric authentication platform
- desktop and mobile-capable access
- fingerprint and facial verification support
- DHA integration for identity verification
- credit bureau integration for demographic, address, employment, and business-interest checks
- banking validation support
- Persal-related payroll integration support
- reporting, dashboards, exception reporting, and audit trails
- training, handover, support, and maintenance

## Key Stakeholders

- Gauteng Provincial Treasury: customer, governance authority, data owner
- Gauteng Provincial Government departments and municipalities: end users and business beneficiaries
- Department of e-Government: security and governance alignment
- SITA: target hosting environment
- Department of Home Affairs: identity-verification source
- credit bureau and banking service providers: external information holders
- GPT IT Services: first-line support audience

## Delivery Model

The project is planned as a two-phase engagement:

- Months 1-12: design, build, integration, testing, deployment, training, and handover
- Months 13-36: support, maintenance, updates, and operational stabilization

This structure aligns to the 36-month contract term described in the tender.

## Bidder Position

Waymark Infotech is positioning the response around a government-controlled, integration-centric verification platform and an agile, prototype-led delivery model. Relevant experience includes SASSA biometric verification using DHA APIs, National Lotteries Commission verification and vetting workflows, biometric voter-registration programs in multiple African countries, and biometrics-driven regulatory registration in telecoms.
