# Customer Profile

## Industry
Public sector government administration and treasury services

## Organization Type
Public sector, highly regulated provincial government institution

## Environment Summary
Gauteng Provincial Treasury (GPT), acting on behalf of Gauteng Provincial Government (GPG), is procuring a web-based biometric authentication system to support recruitment, payroll cleansing, fraud prevention, supply chain screening, and beneficiary verification across multiple provincial departments. The target environment is centrally governed by GPT, supported by the Department of e-Government, integrated with the Department of Home Affairs, credit bureaus, banking-sector services, and Persal, and hosted within a SITA-managed Windows-based environment with strict data ownership, access control, and audit requirements.

## Priority Areas
- security
- compliance
- resilience
- operational control
- identity and demographic verification
- fraud prevention and internal controls
- cross-department integration
- real-time data access and reporting

## Preferred Communication Style
Professional, concise, business-friendly

## Key Customer Outcomes
- Deploy an end-to-end biometric verification platform within 12 months, followed by 2 years of support and maintenance.
- Improve recruitment, payroll, supplier, and beneficiary screening accuracy through real-time identity, banking, address, and mortality checks.
- Reduce fraud risk, adverse audit findings, and manual processing through stronger controls, automation, and decision support.
- Maintain compliance with POPIA, GPT security policies, ISO/IEC 27001-aligned practices, and public-sector governance requirements.
- Preserve government ownership and central control of data, access, backups, disaster recovery, and operational reporting.
